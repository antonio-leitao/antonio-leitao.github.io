About me
I'm a PhD student at the Scuola Normale Superiore and part of the Datashape Team supervised by Nina Otter and Fosca Giannoti working on various projects from topological data analysis, climate science, explainable AI and animal communication.
I am a collaborator at CETI and was previously part of the NPL research lab where I worked with Giovanni Petri .
My main interests are on Topological Data Analysis and Machine Learning.
